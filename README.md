### 2026 paper:
***1. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** "CSQNet: A Query-Driven Channel-Spatial Attention Network for Infrared Small Target Detection" has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (GRSL)** 🎯, 2026.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20GRSL-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：特征并非来自对世界的被动观测，而是在不同表征维度的相互指认中生成。空间与通道不再是独立坐标，而是彼此定义的生成关系。通过查询驱动的交互机制，使不同维度在特征生成过程中参与彼此建模。

### 2025 paper:
***1. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** *"FDPF-Net: A Full-scale Dynamic Pyramid Fusion Network for Infrared Small Target Detection"* has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (TGRS)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20TGRS-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：当个体无法被稳定刻画时，其本质往往隐藏在群体的一致性之中。弱小目标缺乏稳定的共性表征，在跨尺度与多阶段中呈现出一定结构一致性。动态捕捉该类特征将有助于小目标分割。

***2. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** *"Visual Attention-Guided Multi-Perspective Learning and Location-Aware Network for Infrared Small Target Detection"* has been accepted by 🎯 **Pattern Recognition (PR)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Elsevier%20PR-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=elsevier&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：表征空间并非静态分布，而更接近一种能量演化系统。模仿人类多视角对特征张量中的灰度尺度能量分布进行多视角学习，在多视角约束下逐步收敛形成的稳定态。。

***3. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** *"Dual-Pathway Feature Separation and Gated Fusion Network for Infrared Small Target Detection"* has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing (TGRS)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20TGRS-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" /> 
学术观点：不同类型的信息往往源于不同的生成机制，而非同一机制的不同侧面。统一建模带来模糊，分离建模才可能带来清晰的区域响应（显著性特征可视化）与结构表达（轮廓可视化）。

***4. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** "MNHU-Net: A Multi-Scale Feature Fusion and Nested Structure-Based High-Order U-Net for Infrared Small Target Detection" has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Transactions on Aerospace and Electronic Systems (TAES)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20TAES-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：聚焦背景，反而可以分割目标。目标从不孤立存在，其意义由环境赋予。背景不是噪声，而是定义“何为异常”的参照体系。

***5. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** "IIMCNet: Intra- and Inter-modality Correlation Network for Hybrid EEG-fNIRS Brain-Computer Interface" has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics (JBHI)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20JBHI-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：多模态并非简单的信息叠加，而是一种在独立性与一致性之间不断权衡的过程。融合的本质，是对这种张力的调控。

***6. Xiaoyang Yuan, et al.** "OASNet: Orthogonal Attention-Guided Spatial–Semantic Representation Learning Network for Infrared Small Target Detection" has been accepted by 🎯 **IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Letters (GRSL)** 🎯, 2025.  <img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/IEEE%20GRSL-2025%20Accepted-darkred?style=flat&logo=ieee&logoColor=white" width="150" style="vertical-align: middle;" />  
学术观点：X-Y-Z三个相互垂直轴构成一个基本的张量空间，高维表征的复杂性并不源于信息不足，而源于耦合过度。理解一个系统，往往始于将其拆解为彼此正交的生成因素。
